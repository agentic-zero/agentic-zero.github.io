# SOP — AI Planning — Risk Assessment and Objectives
**Process ID:** ISO42001-6
**Framework:** ISO 42001:2023 | **Domain:** ISO 42001
**Generated:** 2026-06-10

## Purpose
AI-specific risk and impact assessment, AI system objectives, planning for AI system changes including bias risk, safety risk, security risk and societal impact assessment

## Triggers
- New AI use case added to register
- Planned AI system change detected
- Quarterly compliance audit scheduled

## Inputs Required
- AI use case register
- risk assessment methodology
- AI objectives
- impact assessment frameworks
- bias testing data

## Process Steps
1. IF bias_metric_score > 0.3 THEN require additional BiasAssessment
2. IF societal_impact detected THEN add to ImpactAssessment
3. IF automation_potential < 0.5 THEN require manual review before RiskTreatmentPlan approval

## Expected Outputs
- AI risk register
- impact assessments
- bias assessments
- AI objectives
- risk treatment plans

## Business Rules
- All outputs must reference ISO 42001:2023 Clause 6
- EU AI Act Art.9 risk management must be mapped to every AIRiskRegister entry
- bias_metric_scores must be computed before BiasAssessment is finalized
- AIObjectives must be versioned on every change

## Exception Handling
- If sector not in manufacturing/pharma/defense/chemical/food/automotive/distribution then skip sector-specific bias testing and log compliance_flag only
- If related_process ISO42001-5 unavailable then use fallback risk assessment methodology

## Success Criteria
- AI risk identification rate >= 0.9
- impact assessment coverage == 100%
- bias metric scores below threshold for all protected groups
- All AIObjectives have documented achievement evidence

## Compliance Requirements
- ISO 42001:2023 Clause 6
- EU AI Act Art.9 risk management
- NIST AI RMF Map
- algorithmic impact assessment
