# SOP — AI Performance Evaluation — Monitoring and Audit
**Process ID:** ISO42001-9
**Framework:** ISO 42001:2023 | **Domain:** ISO 42001
**Generated:** 2026-06-10

## Purpose
Monitoring and measurement of AI system performance including model drift detection, bias monitoring, fairness metrics, internal AI audit program and management review of the AIMS

## Triggers
- cron job every 7 days for scheduled monitoring
- new Incident_Data record received
- Stakeholder_Feedback submission via portal
- quarterly calendar event for AIMS_Management_Review

## Inputs Required
- AI performance data
- bias metrics
- fairness indicators
- audit findings
- incident data
- stakeholder feedback

## Process Steps
1. IF model_accuracy < 0.92 OR bias_metric_score > 0.15 THEN trigger Model_Drift_Detector and escalate to AIMS_Management_Review
2. IF audit_completion_rate < 1.0 THEN schedule immediate AI_Audit_Report generation
3. IF fairness_indicators deviate > 10% from baseline THEN create Improvement_Action with 30-day deadline

## Expected Outputs
- AI performance reports
- bias monitoring reports
- AI audit reports
- AIMS management review
- improvement actions

## Business Rules
- All AI_Performance_Data must be logged per EU AI Act Art.12 with timestamp and model version
- Bias_Metrics and Fairness_Indicators must be computed at minimum weekly frequency
- AIMS_Management_Review must occur at least quarterly with documented minutes and assigned Improvement_Action owners
- Audit completion rate KPI must equal 100% per quarter

## Exception Handling
- If input data volume < 1000 records then skip automated bias calculation and flag for manual review within 48 hours
- If regulatory access is revoked then pause automated monitoring and route to compliance team

## Success Criteria
- All KPIs (model_accuracy, bias_metric_score, fairness_indicators, audit_completion_rate) within defined thresholds
- AI_Performance_Report, Bias_Monitoring_Report and AI_Audit_Report generated and stored
- AIMS_Management_Review completed with signed minutes and at least one Improvement_Action created

## Compliance Requirements
- ISO 42001:2023 Clause 9
- EU AI Act Art.12 logging
- EU AI Act Art.72 monitoring
- NIST AI RMF Measure
