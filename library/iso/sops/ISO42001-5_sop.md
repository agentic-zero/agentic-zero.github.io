# SOP — AI Leadership — Policy and Governance
**Process ID:** ISO42001-5
**Framework:** ISO 42001:2023 | **Domain:** ISO 42001
**Generated:** 2026-06-10

## Purpose
Leadership commitment to responsible AI, AI policy establishment, organizational roles for AI governance including AI ethics board, AI risk owner and AI system owners

## Triggers
- Related process ISO42001-4 completion signal
- Annual leadership review cycle start
- New regulatory_requirement published

## Inputs Required
- AI strategy
- AI risk appetite
- regulatory requirements
- ethical principles
- organizational structure

## Process Steps
1. IF leadership_commitment == true AND regulatory_requirements reviewed THEN establish AI_Policy
2. IF organizational_structure updated THEN assign AI_Risk_Owner and AI_System_Owner roles

## Expected Outputs
- AI policy
- AI governance framework
- AI ethics principles
- role assignments
- AI accountability matrix

## Business Rules
- AI_Policy must be approved by Leadership before publication
- AI_Accountability_Matrix must cover all AI systems with named owners
- Governance meetings must occur at minimum quarterly frequency

## Exception Handling
- IF sector not in applicability list THEN skip sector-specific ethical principles but retain core ISO 42001 Clause 5 requirements

## Success Criteria
- AI_policy_communication_rate >= 0.95
- governance_meeting_frequency >= 4 per year
- AI_accountability_coverage == 1.0 for all AI systems

## Compliance Requirements
- ISO 42001:2023 Clause 5
- EU AI Act Art.4 AI literacy
- corporate AI governance
