# SOP — AI Performance Evaluation — Monitoring and Audit
**Process ID:** ISO42001-9
**Framework:** ISO 42001:2023 | **Domain:** ISO 42001
**Generated:** 2026-06-12

## Purpose
Monitoring and measurement of AI system performance including model drift detection, bias monitoring, fairness metrics, internal AI audit program and management review of the AIMS

## Triggers
- Scheduled quarterly monitoring cycle
- Receipt of new Incident_Data or Stakeholder_Feedback exceeding threshold
- External compliance audit request

## Inputs Required
- AI performance data
- bias metrics
- fairness indicators
- audit findings
- incident data
- stakeholder feedback

## Process Steps
1. IF model_accuracy < 0.92 OR bias_metric_score > 0.15 THEN trigger Improvement_Action
2. IF audit_completion_rate < 1.0 THEN schedule additional AI_Audit_Report within 30 days

## Expected Outputs
- AI performance reports
- bias monitoring reports
- AI audit reports
- AIMS management review
- improvement actions

## Business Rules
- All KPIs must be logged with timestamp and source per EU AI Act Art.12
- Internal AI audit program must execute at minimum quarterly frequency
- AIMS_Management_Review must occur at least annually with documented stakeholder sign-off

## Exception Handling
- If data source for Bias_Metrics is unavailable for >7 days, substitute with synthetic fairness indicators and flag in AI_Audit_Report
- If incident_data volume exceeds 1000 events/day, escalate directly to AIMS_Management_Review bypassing standard reporting

## Success Criteria
- AIMS_effectiveness >= 0.95
- All bias_metric_scores within defined fairness thresholds
- 100% audit_completion_rate with zero open findings >30 days

## Compliance Requirements
- ISO 42001:2023 Clause 9
- EU AI Act Art.12 logging
- EU AI Act Art.72 monitoring
- NIST AI RMF Measure
