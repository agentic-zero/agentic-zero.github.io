# SOP — Pack and Prepare Products for Distribution
**Process ID:** SCOR-M5.1
**Framework:** SCOR | **Domain:** Make
**Generated:** 2026-06-04

## Purpose
Process of packing and preparing products for distribution to customers

## Triggers
- tested and inspected products are available
- customer order is received

## Inputs Required
- tested and inspected products
- packaging materials

## Process Steps
1. IF Product is damaged THEN return to inspection
2. IF Packaging Materials are insufficient THEN order more

## Expected Outputs
- packed and prepared products
- shipping documents

## Business Rules
- rule1: Packaging must comply with GDP regulations if distribution
- rule2: Shipping accuracy must be above 95%

## Exception Handling
- exception1: Insufficient Packaging Materials - order more and wait for delivery
- exception2: Product damage during packing - return to inspection and re-pack

## Success Criteria
- Product is packed and prepared for distribution within 24 hours
- Shipping accuracy is above 95%

## Compliance Requirements
- GDP if distribution
