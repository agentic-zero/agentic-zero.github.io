# SOP — Improvement — Nonconformity and Continual Improvement
**Process ID:** ISO9001-10
**Framework:** ISO 9001:2015 | **Domain:** ISO 9001
**Generated:** 2026-06-12

## Purpose
Management of nonconformities and corrective actions, continual improvement of the QMS suitability adequacy and effectiveness

## Triggers
- new nonconformance report submitted
- audit finding published
- customer complaint logged
- performance gap detected via KPI threshold breach

## Inputs Required
- nonconformance reports
- audit findings
- customer complaints
- performance gaps
- improvement opportunities

## Process Steps
1. IF nonconformance report received THEN create CorrectiveAction record with due date
2. IF recurrence rate > 5% THEN escalate to ImprovementProject
3. IF audit finding severity = critical THEN trigger immediate CAPA within 24 hours

## Expected Outputs
- corrective actions
- improvement projects
- updated procedures
- lessons learned
- QMS improvements

## Business Rules
- Every Nonconformity must have at least one CorrectiveAction assigned within 48 hours
- CorrectiveAction must include root cause analysis before closure
- All ImprovementProject must be linked to at least one KPI target
- LessonsLearned must be reviewed before any QMS procedure update

## Exception Handling
- IF regulatory deadline conflicts with standard cycle time THEN override cycle time with documented justification
- IF customer complaint involves safety issue THEN bypass normal triage and escalate directly to management review

## Success Criteria
- corrective_action closed within target cycle time
- recurrence rate reduced by >= 20% within 90 days
- improvement project completion rate >= 85%
- QMS effectiveness score increased or maintained

## Compliance Requirements
- ISO 9001:2015 Clause 10
- CAPA requirements
- continual improvement mandate
