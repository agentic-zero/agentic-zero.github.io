# SOP — Analyze Supply Chain Capabilities and Capacity
**Process ID:** SCOR-P1.2
**Framework:** SCOR | **Domain:** Plan
**Generated:** 2026-06-01

## Purpose
Process of analyzing supply chain capabilities and capacity to meet demand

## Triggers
- trigger1: changes in Demand or Supply Chain Requirements
- trigger2: supplier performance issues or non-compliance

## Inputs Required
- supply chain requirements
- capacity data
- supplier data

## Process Steps
1. IF Demand exceeds Capacity THEN trigger Capacity Expansion
2. IF Supplier performance is low THEN consider alternative Suppliers

## Expected Outputs
- supply chain capacity plan
- capability analysis

## Business Rules
- rule1: Supply Chain Capacity must meet or exceed Demand
- rule2: Suppliers must meet compliance flags (GxP if pharma, GDP if distribution)

## Exception Handling
- exception1: Insufficient Capacity - trigger emergency sourcing or outsourcing
- exception2: Supplier non-compliance - trigger supplier audit and potential termination

## Success Criteria
- success1: Supply Chain Capacity meets or exceeds Demand
- success2: Suppliers meet compliance flags and performance metrics

## Compliance Requirements
- GxP if pharma
- GDP if distribution
