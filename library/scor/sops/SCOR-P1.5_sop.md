# SOP — Manage Supply Chain Risk
**Process ID:** SCOR-P1.5
**Framework:** SCOR | **Domain:** Plan
**Generated:** 2026-06-01

## Purpose
Process of identifying and mitigating supply chain risks

## Triggers
- change in supply chain requirements
- new supplier onboarding
- regular risk assessment schedule

## Inputs Required
- supply chain requirements
- risk data
- supplier data

## Process Steps
1. IF Risk Exposure is high THEN implement Mitigation Plan
2. IF Supplier data indicates high risk THEN reassess Risk Exposure

## Expected Outputs
- risk assessment
- mitigation plan

## Business Rules
- rule1: Risk Assessment must be performed regularly
- rule2: Mitigation Plan must be based on Risk Assessment
- rule3: Supplier data must be up-to-date and accurate

## Exception Handling
- IF no Supplier data is available THEN use industry benchmarks
- IF Risk Assessment indicates extreme risk THEN escalate to management

## Success Criteria
- Risk Exposure is reduced
- Mitigation Plan is implemented and effective
- Supply Chain operations are stable and reliable

## Compliance Requirements
- GxP if pharma
- GDP if distribution
