FAQ
What happens in the free AUDIT?
We map your process, ERP, volume and team. You get a detailed ROI calculation with your real data before signing anything. The AUDIT takes 30 minutes and the report arrives within 24 hours. No commitment required.
What does "certified" mean for the agent?
Every agent passes the Guardian protocol: EU AI Act risk classification, ISO 42001 alignment, NIST AI RMF scoring, and GDPR checks. You receive a signed certificate with scores, conditions, and a remediation plan before deployment.
Which ERPs do you integrate with?
Essential covers SAP ECC, SAP HANA, SAP S/4HANA, Oracle, MS Dynamics 365, Business Central, NetSuite, Odoo, Infor, and Epicor. Standard and Enterprise add custom, legacy, and on-premise systems via RFC/BAPI, REST, SOAP, or direct database connectors.
Can I start with Essential and upgrade?
Yes. Essential is designed as the entry point. When your team is ready to automate additional processes or move to a multi-agent architecture, upgrading is seamless — your certified agent remains in production while the new processes are built and certified in parallel.
What is the CooperBench Shield?
An architectural protection layer based on Stanford HAI ICLR 2026 research. It prevents coordination degradation in multi-agent systems — without it, swarms lose ~50% capacity when agents coordinate. Standard and Enterprise tiers include it from day one.
What if the ROI does not materialise?
If the measured ROI at 30 / 60 / 90 days falls more than 20% below the AUDIT estimate, we rebuild the agent at no additional cost until the threshold is reached — or apply a proportional credit to your subscription.
