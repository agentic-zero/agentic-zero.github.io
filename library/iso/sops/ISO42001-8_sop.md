# SOP — AI Operation — System Lifecycle and Controls
**Process ID:** ISO42001-8
**Framework:** ISO 42001:2023 | **Domain:** ISO 42001
**Generated:** 2026-06-12

## Purpose
Operational control of AI systems throughout their lifecycle including development controls, validation, deployment controls, human oversight mechanisms, incident management and supply chain AI controls

## Triggers
- New AI system designs and validation protocols received
- Deployment plans submitted for operational control
- Supplier AI assessments updated

## Inputs Required
- AI system designs
- validation protocols
- deployment plans
- human oversight rules
- supplier AI assessments

## Process Steps
1. IF AI validation pass rate >= 0.95 THEN proceed to deployment ELSE return to redesign
2. IF human oversight compliance < 1.0 THEN block deployment
3. IF supplier AI assessment score < threshold THEN reject supplier integration

## Expected Outputs
- validated AI systems
- deployment records
- human oversight logs
- incident reports
- supplier AI assessments

## Business Rules
- All AI operations must produce human oversight logs before deployment
- Incident reports must be generated within 24 hours of detection
- Supplier AI assessments required for every external model or component
- GxP computer validation mandatory for pharma sector deployments

## Exception Handling
- Defense sector: add classified data handling override with dual authorization
- Pharma sector: require additional GxP validation step before KPI logging

## Success Criteria
- AI validation pass rate >= 0.95
- human oversight compliance == 1.0
- deployment success rate >= 0.98
- AI incident rate <= 0.01

## Compliance Requirements
- ISO 42001:2023 Clause 8
- EU AI Act Art.9-14 operations
- NIST AI RMF Manage
- GxP computer validation if pharma
