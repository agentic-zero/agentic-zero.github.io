# SOP — AI Operation — System Lifecycle and Controls
**Process ID:** ISO42001-8
**Framework:** ISO 42001:2023 | **Domain:** ISO 42001
**Generated:** 2026-06-10

## Purpose
Operational control of AI systems throughout their lifecycle including development controls, validation, deployment controls, human oversight mechanisms, incident management and supply chain AI controls

## Triggers
- New AI_System design received from ISO42001-7
- Updated Deployment_Plan submitted
- Supplier_AI_Assessment updated

## Inputs Required
- AI system designs
- validation protocols
- deployment plans
- human oversight rules
- supplier AI assessments

## Process Steps
1. IF KPI_Validation_Pass_Rate < 0.95 THEN block deployment and require re-validation
2. IF KPI_Human_Oversight_Compliance < 1.0 THEN escalate to human review before deployment
3. IF KPI_Incident_Rate > 0.02 THEN initiate incident management and pause related AI systems

## Expected Outputs
- validated AI systems
- deployment records
- human oversight logs
- incident reports
- supplier AI assessments

## Business Rules
- Every AI_System must produce Human_Oversight_Log before deployment
- Supplier_AI_Assessment required for all external components in AI_System
- All outputs must be logged with timestamp and responsible agent ID
- GxP validation required if sector is pharma

## Exception Handling
- IF automation_potential < 0.5 THEN require manual approval for deployment
- IF sector is defense THEN add extra compliance check for EU AI Act Art.9-14

## Success Criteria
- KPI_Validation_Pass_Rate >= 0.95 AND KPI_Deployment_Success_Rate >= 0.98 AND KPI_Incident_Rate <= 0.01

## Compliance Requirements
- ISO 42001:2023 Clause 8
- EU AI Act Art.9-14 operations
- NIST AI RMF Manage
- GxP computer validation if pharma
