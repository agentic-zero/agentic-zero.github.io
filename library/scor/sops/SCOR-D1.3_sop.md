# SOP — Design Product and Service Offerings
**Process ID:** SCOR-D1.3
**Framework:** SCOR-D | **Domain:** Design Chain
**Generated:** 2026-06-05

## Purpose
Process of designing new product and service offerings, including the development of product specifications and design documentation

## Triggers
- New product or service offering is proposed
- Change in Customer Requirements or Market Trends

## Inputs Required
- customer requirements
- market trends
- technological advancements

## Process Steps
1. IF Customer Requirements are incomplete THEN request additional information
2. IF Design Quality does not meet standards THEN revise Product Design

## Expected Outputs
- product design
- service design
- design documentation

## Business Rules
- Design Quality must meet industry standards
- Time-to-Market must be within specified timeframe
- Customer Satisfaction must be measured and reported

## Exception Handling
- IF GxP compliance is required THEN ensure design meets regulatory requirements
- IF EU AI Act applies THEN ensure AI-driven design meets transparency and accountability standards

## Success Criteria
- Product Design meets Customer Requirements and industry standards
- Service Design meets Customer Requirements and industry standards
- Design Documentation is complete and accurate

## Compliance Requirements
- GxP if pharma or medical devices
- EU AI Act if AI-driven design
