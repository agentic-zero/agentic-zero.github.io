# SOP — AI Improvement — Nonconformity and Continual Improvement
**Process ID:** ISO42001-10
**Framework:** ISO 42001:2023 | **Domain:** ISO 42001
**Generated:** 2026-06-10

## Purpose
Management of AI-specific nonconformities including model failures, bias incidents, safety events and continual improvement of the AI management system

## Triggers
- New NonconformanceReport received
- BiasIncident logged
- SafetyEvent detected
- AuditFinding published
- PerformanceGap threshold exceeded

## Inputs Required
- AI nonconformance reports
- bias incidents
- safety events
- audit findings
- performance gaps

## Process Steps
1. IF incident recurrence rate > 0.05 THEN execute ModelRetrainingTrigger
2. IF corrective action cycle time > 14 days THEN escalate to AIMSImprovement

## Expected Outputs
- AI corrective actions
- model retraining triggers
- AIMS improvements
- lessons learned
- updated AI controls

## Business Rules
- Every NonconformanceReport must be logged with timestamp and source within 24 hours
- All CorrectiveAction records require linked KPI metrics for closure
- ModelRetrainingTrigger must reference at least one BiasIncident or SafetyEvent

## Exception Handling
- SafetyEvent with critical severity bypasses standard cycle time and requires immediate CorrectiveAction within 4 hours
- AuditFinding from external regulator overrides internal PerformanceGap priority

## Success Criteria
- AI corrective action cycle time <= KPI target
- model incident recurrence rate decreased by >= 20% quarter-over-quarter
- AIMSImprovement implemented and verified in next audit

## Compliance Requirements
- ISO 42001:2023 Clause 10
- EU AI Act post-market monitoring
- NIST AI RMF continual improvement
