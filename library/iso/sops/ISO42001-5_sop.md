# SOP — AI Leadership — Policy and Governance
**Process ID:** ISO42001-5
**Framework:** ISO 42001:2023 | **Domain:** ISO 42001
**Generated:** 2026-06-10

## Purpose
Leadership commitment to responsible AI, AI policy establishment, organizational roles for AI governance including AI ethics board, AI risk owner and AI system owners

## Triggers
- New AI_Strategy document published
- Regulatory_Requirements change detected via compliance feed
- Annual ISO 42001 audit scheduled

## Inputs Required
- AI strategy
- AI risk appetite
- regulatory requirements
- ethical principles
- organizational structure

## Process Steps
1. IF regulatory_requirements updated THEN trigger AI_Policy revision within 30 days
2. IF governance_meeting_frequency < 1 per quarter THEN escalate to AI_Leadership for review

## Expected Outputs
- AI policy
- AI governance framework
- AI ethics principles
- role assignments
- AI accountability matrix

## Business Rules
- AI_Policy must be approved by AI_Leadership before publication
- AI_Accountability_Matrix must assign at least one owner per AI system
- All role_assignments require documented acceptance by assignee

## Exception Handling
- If sector is defense and classified data involved then bypass public AI_policy communication_rate KPI and log under restricted access only

## Success Criteria
- AI_policy_communication_rate >= 95%
- governance_meeting_frequency >= 4 per year
- AI_accountability_coverage == 100%

## Compliance Requirements
- ISO 42001:2023 Clause 5
- EU AI Act Art.4 AI literacy
- corporate AI governance
