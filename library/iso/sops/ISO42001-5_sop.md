# SOP — AI Leadership — Policy and Governance
**Process ID:** ISO42001-5
**Framework:** ISO 42001:2023 | **Domain:** ISO 42001
**Generated:** 2026-06-10

## Purpose
Leadership commitment to responsible AI, AI policy establishment, organizational roles for AI governance including AI ethics board, AI risk owner and AI system owners

## Triggers
- AI_strategy approval
- regulatory_update event
- annual governance review date

## Inputs Required
- AI strategy
- AI risk appetite
- regulatory requirements
- ethical principles
- organizational structure

## Process Steps
1. IF regulatory_requirements updated THEN revise AI_Policy within 30 days
2. IF AI_risk_appetite changed THEN reassign AI_Risk_Owner

## Expected Outputs
- AI policy
- AI governance framework
- AI ethics principles
- role assignments
- AI accountability matrix

## Business Rules
- AI_Policy must be approved by AI_Leadership before publication
- Governance meetings must occur at minimum quarterly
- AI_Accountability_Matrix must assign every AI system to an owner
- AI policy communication rate must exceed 95%

## Exception Handling
- If headcount < 50 then AI_Ethics_Board may be merged with existing ethics committee with documented justification

## Success Criteria
- AI policy communication rate >= 95%
- governance meeting frequency >= 4 per year
- AI accountability coverage == 100%

## Compliance Requirements
- ISO 42001:2023 Clause 5
- EU AI Act Art.4 AI literacy
- corporate AI governance
