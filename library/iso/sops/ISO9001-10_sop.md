# SOP — Improvement — Nonconformity and Continual Improvement
**Process ID:** ISO9001-10
**Framework:** ISO 9001:2015 | **Domain:** ISO 9001
**Generated:** 2026-06-10

## Purpose
Management of nonconformities and corrective actions, continual improvement of the QMS suitability adequacy and effectiveness

## Triggers
- new NonconformanceReport received
- AuditFinding with severity >= medium
- CustomerComplaint unresolved after 7 days

## Inputs Required
- nonconformance reports
- audit findings
- customer complaints
- performance gaps
- improvement opportunities

## Process Steps
1. IF recurrence_rate > 0.15 THEN create ImprovementProject
2. IF corrective_action_cycle_time > 30_days THEN escalate to management_review

## Expected Outputs
- corrective actions
- improvement projects
- updated procedures
- lessons learned
- QMS improvements

## Business Rules
- Every NonconformanceReport must have root_cause documented before CorrectiveAction closure
- All CorrectiveAction records must link to at least one KPI measurement

## Exception Handling
- Critical safety nonconformance: bypass standard cycle_time and trigger immediate containment within 24 hours

## Success Criteria
- recurrence_rate decreased by >= 20% quarter-over-quarter
- improvement_project_completion >= 90% of planned milestones

## Compliance Requirements
- ISO 9001:2015 Clause 10
- CAPA requirements
- continual improvement mandate
