# SOP — Risk Management Principles and Framework
**Process ID:** ISO31000-P1
**Framework:** ISO 31000:2018 | **Domain:** ISO 31000
**Generated:** 2026-06-10

## Purpose
Establishment of risk management principles and framework including leadership commitment, integration, design, implementation, evaluation and improvement of risk management across the organization

## Triggers
- new regulatory requirement published
- annual strategy review cycle start
- stakeholder_requirements update event

## Inputs Required
- organizational context
- risk appetite
- stakeholder requirements
- existing risk practices
- regulatory requirements

## Process Steps
1. IF leadership_engagement_rate < 0.8 THEN escalate to board for recommitment
2. IF integration_coverage < 0.9 THEN trigger gap remediation plan

## Expected Outputs
- risk management framework
- risk management policy
- integration plan
- mandate and commitment

## Business Rules
- RiskManagementFramework must cover all sector_applicability domains listed
- MandateAndCommitment must be signed by C-level executive before framework deployment
- Framework maturity score must be recalculated quarterly

## Exception Handling
- If regulatory_requirements conflict with risk_appetite, regulatory_requirements take precedence and risk_appetite is updated within 30 days

## Success Criteria
- framework_maturity_score >= 4.0 AND integration_coverage >= 0.95 AND all outputs generated with approval timestamps

## Compliance Requirements
- ISO 31000:2018
- enterprise risk management
- corporate governance
