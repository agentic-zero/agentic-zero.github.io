# SOP — Develop Design Chain Roadmap
**Process ID:** SCOR-D1.2
**Framework:** SCOR-D | **Domain:** Design Chain
**Generated:** 2026-06-04

## Purpose
Process of creating a detailed roadmap for the design chain, including key milestones and deliverables

## Triggers
- Design Chain Strategy is updated
- Design Chain Objectives are updated
- Resource Availability changes
- Project Schedule is due for review

## Inputs Required
- design chain strategy
- design chain objectives
- resource availability

## Process Steps
1. IF Design Chain Strategy is updated THEN update Design Chain Roadmap
2. IF Resource Availability changes THEN update Design Chain Roadmap
3. IF Milestone Achievement is not met THEN re-evaluate Design Chain Roadmap

## Expected Outputs
- design chain roadmap
- project schedule

## Business Rules
- Design Chain Roadmap must be reviewed and updated annually
- Design Chain Roadmap must align with Design Chain Objectives
- Resource Availability must be considered when creating Design Chain Roadmap

## Exception Handling
- IF Design Chain Roadmap is not achievable THEN escalate to management and re-evaluate Design Chain Strategy
- IF Resource Availability is insufficient THEN identify alternative resources or adjust Design Chain Roadmap

## Success Criteria
- Design Chain Roadmap is completed and approved
- Milestones are achieved within scheduled timeframe
- Resource Utilization is within planned limits

## Compliance Requirements
- GxP if pharma or medical devices
