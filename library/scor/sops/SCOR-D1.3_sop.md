# SOP — Design Product and Service Offerings
**Process ID:** SCOR-D1.3
**Framework:** SCOR-D | **Domain:** Design Chain
**Generated:** 2026-06-06

## Purpose
Process of designing new product and service offerings, including the development of product specifications and design documentation

## Triggers
- New Customer Requirements are received
- Market Trends indicate a need for new Product Design
- Technological Advancements enable new Service Design

## Inputs Required
- customer requirements
- market trends
- technological advancements

## Process Steps
1. IF Design Quality is below threshold THEN revise Product Design
2. IF Time-to-Market exceeds target THEN prioritize Service Design

## Expected Outputs
- product design
- service design
- design documentation

## Business Rules
- Design Quality must meet customer requirements
- Time-to-Market must be within target range
- Design Documentation must be complete and accurate

## Exception Handling
- IF Customer Requirements change THEN update Product Design and Service Design
- IF Technological Advancements render Design obsolete THEN revise Design Documentation

## Success Criteria
- Design Quality meets customer requirements
- Time-to-Market is within target range
- Customer Satisfaction is above threshold

## Compliance Requirements
- GxP if pharma or medical devices
- EU AI Act if AI-driven design
