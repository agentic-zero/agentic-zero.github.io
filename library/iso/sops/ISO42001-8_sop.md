# SOP — AI Operation — System Lifecycle and Controls
**Process ID:** ISO42001-8
**Framework:** ISO 42001:2023 | **Domain:** ISO 42001
**Generated:** 2026-06-10

## Purpose
Operational control of AI systems throughout their lifecycle including development controls, validation, deployment controls, human oversight mechanisms, incident management and supply chain AI controls

## Triggers
- Receipt of AI system designs and validation protocols from ISO42001-7
- Completion of supplier AI assessments
- Scheduled quarterly lifecycle review

## Inputs Required
- AI system designs
- validation protocols
- deployment plans
- human oversight rules
- supplier AI assessments

## Process Steps
1. IF AI_validation_pass_rate >= 0.95 THEN proceed to deployment ELSE trigger re-validation
2. IF human_oversight_compliance < 1.0 THEN halt deployment and log exception
3. IF AI_incident_rate > 0.02 THEN activate incident_management and notify related_process ISO42001-9

## Expected Outputs
- validated AI systems
- deployment records
- human oversight logs
- incident reports
- supplier AI assessments

## Business Rules
- All AI systems must execute validation protocols before deployment per ISO 42001 Clause 8
- Human oversight logs must be generated for every operational decision with timestamp and actor ID
- Supplier_AI_Assessment must be refreshed every 90 days or on material change
- GxP computer validation required if sector is pharma

## Exception Handling
- Validation fails due to data drift: re-run protocol with updated dataset and extend oversight period by 30 days
- Supplier assessment unavailable: apply temporary manual oversight rule and block automated deployment

## Success Criteria
- AI_validation_pass_rate >= 0.95 AND deployment_success_rate >= 0.98 AND AI_incident_rate <= 0.01 over 30-day window

## Compliance Requirements
- ISO 42001:2023 Clause 8
- EU AI Act Art.9-14 operations
- NIST AI RMF Manage
- GxP computer validation if pharma
