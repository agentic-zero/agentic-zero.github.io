# SOP — Define Design Chain Strategy
**Process ID:** SCOR-D1.1
**Framework:** SCOR-D | **Domain:** Design Chain
**Generated:** 2026-06-05

## Purpose
Process of defining the overall design chain strategy and objectives

## Triggers
- Change in Business Objectives
- Change in Customer Requirements
- Change in Market Trends
- Project initiation

## Inputs Required
- business objectives
- customer requirements
- market trends

## Process Steps
1. IF Business Objectives change THEN re-evaluate Design Chain Strategy
2. IF Customer Requirements change THEN re-evaluate Design Chain Strategy
3. IF Market Trends change THEN re-evaluate Design Chain Strategy

## Expected Outputs
- design chain strategy
- design chain objectives

## Business Rules
- Design Chain Strategy must align with Business Objectives
- Design Chain Strategy must meet Customer Requirements
- Design Chain Strategy must consider Market Trends
- Design Chain Strategy must comply with EU AI Act if AI-driven design is used

## Exception Handling
- IF Design Chain Strategy development is delayed THEN notify stakeholders and re-evaluate project timeline
- IF Design Chain Strategy is not aligned with Business Objectives THEN re-evaluate and revise Design Chain Strategy

## Success Criteria
- Design Chain Strategy is aligned with Business Objectives
- Design Chain Strategy meets Customer Requirements
- Design Chain Strategy considers Market Trends
- Time-to-Market is within target range
- Design Quality meets target range
- Cost of Design is within target range

## Compliance Requirements
- EU AI Act if AI-driven design
