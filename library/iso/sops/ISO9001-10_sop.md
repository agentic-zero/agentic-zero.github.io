# SOP — Improvement — Nonconformity and Continual Improvement
**Process ID:** ISO9001-10
**Framework:** ISO 9001:2015 | **Domain:** ISO 9001
**Generated:** 2026-06-10

## Purpose
Management of nonconformities and corrective actions, continual improvement of the QMS suitability adequacy and effectiveness

## Triggers
- new nonconformance_report received
- audit_finding published
- customer_complaint logged
- performance_gap detected via KPI

## Inputs Required
- nonconformance reports
- audit findings
- customer complaints
- performance gaps
- improvement opportunities

## Process Steps
1. IF nonconformity.severity == 'critical' THEN execute CorrectiveAction within 24h
2. IF recurrence_rate > 0.05 THEN initiate ImprovementProject
3. IF corrective_action.effectiveness_review == 'failed' THEN reopen Nonconformity

## Expected Outputs
- corrective actions
- improvement projects
- updated procedures
- lessons learned
- QMS improvements

## Business Rules
- Every Nonconformity must have documented root_cause and CorrectiveAction before closure
- CorrectiveAction must include effectiveness_review within 30 days of implementation
- All ImprovementProject must be linked to at least one KPI target

## Exception Handling
- Minor nonconformity (severity == 'low' AND no customer impact) may skip full root_cause analysis and use simplified correction only
- Regulatory CAPA requirements override automation_potential when compliance_flags include 'CAPA requirements'

## Success Criteria
- corrective_action.cycle_time <= target AND recurrence_rate <= 0.05 AND improvement_project.completion_rate >= 0.9

## Compliance Requirements
- ISO 9001:2015 Clause 10
- CAPA requirements
- continual improvement mandate
